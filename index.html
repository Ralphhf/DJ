About

Max Voltage is an internationally acclaimed electronic music producer and DJ, known for electrifying performances and groundbreaking productions that push the boundaries of electronic music.


With over a decade of experience in the industry, Max has headlined major festivals worldwide, from Tomorrowland to Ultra Music Festival, bringing unique energy and innovative sound design to dance floors across the globe.


Specializing in progressive house, techno, and future bass, Max's signature sound combines powerful basslines, ethereal melodies, and cutting-edge production techniques that create an immersive sonic experience.
